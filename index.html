Upload images, arrange them, and export your collage.

Shortcuts: Ctrl/Cmd+Z undo, Ctrl/Cmd+Y redo, Ctrl/Cmd+S save project, Ctrl/Cmd+E export. Mouse: drag image to move, wheel to resize, Shift+drag image to resize. Tile keyboard: arrows move image, Shift+arrows move faster, +/- zoom, Alt+arrow reorders, Delete removes.

Canvas: 2000 x 2000
